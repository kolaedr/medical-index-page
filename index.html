WE HAVE THE BEST COSMETIC AND ORTHOPEDIC PRICES YOU CAN FIND!

Medical supplies, especially cosmetic and orthopedic injectables, can be costly. Many clinics and doctors like you ask themselves, where can I get the medical supplies I need for lower prices? We started MedicalSpaRX.com to fill this need. MedicalSpaRX is a well-established international medical supply company offering savings on individual products and special group buy discounts.

New customers often ask us, how do you provide the same products as I buy locally but at much better prices? Since we source our cosmetic and orthopedic products from countries with government-regulated prices, we purchase the products for less and pass the savings on to medical clinics like you. We believe that helping your patients achieve their cosmetic goals should not break the bank.

We can help you and your patients with the lowest possible prices on cosmetic injectables such as SCULPTRA®, JUVÉDERM®, RADIESSE®, TEOSYAL®, RESTYLANE®, RESTYLANE® PERLANE®, BELOTERO® and Surgiderm. We also sell orthopedic injectables such as ORTHOVISC®, SYNVISC®, and HYALGAN®.

If you want to save even more, you should ask us, what is the Group Buy? When you choose our Group Buy option, you can order just the amount of product you need at a fraction of the cost, just as if you were buying in bulk. So when will your order ship out? When enough other buyers have purchased the same product through the Group Buy to meet our minimum volume requirement. Your credit card will be charged when you place your order, and we will give you updates as you wait for your order to arrive. The Group Buy savings option can take longer, so if you do need to get your order immediately, you should place your order through our usual method at our regular low prices.

You may ask, who can buy cosmetic and orthopedic medical supplies from MedicalSpaRX? We only sell our products to medical practitioners, clinics, hospitals, pharmacies and other medical establishments with a valid license.

When you buy your cosmetic and orthopedic supplies from MedicalSpaRX.com you get quality medical products at good prices. Why not register for free and start saving today?
